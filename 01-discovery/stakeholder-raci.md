# Stakeholder RACI Matrix

| Activity | Engineering Lead | Security | Legal | IT Admin | Executive Sponsor |
|----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| License procurement | I | I | C | I | A/R |
| SSO/SCIM configuration | C | I | — | R | A |
| AI usage policy approval | I | R | R | I | A |
| Content exclusion rules | R | A | C | I | I |
| Pilot team selection | R | I | — | I | A |
| Seat assignment | C | I | — | R | A |
| Developer training | R | I | — | C | I |
| Usage reporting/ROI | R | I | — | C | A |
| Security review (code suggestions) | C | R/A | C | — | I |

**Legend:** R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed
